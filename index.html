ABOUT ME

I love science & technology

Science has always been a fascinating subject to me, one that I was quite good at too. So, naturally I followed its path. My academic path and aptitute for science has led me to obtain my doctorate degree in the Genetics of Ageing at the age of 25. I'm now a Research Fellow, a natural scientific career progression which one day I hope to culminate with me supervising my own group of scientists working towards an era of a healthier and disease-free living. I say this with my own tint of transhumanism, the idea that one day technology & science together will help eradicate ageing, one of the most savage diseases known to all living organisms. Here, you will be able to find more about myself & current endeavours. Enjoy!
